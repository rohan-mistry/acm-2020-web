ABOUT LOC

One of the most awaited events of DJ-ACM,LoC(Lines of Code) is around the corner. With over 200 participants from numerous colleges, the hackathon is a gathering where individual programmers collaborate their codes to make industry-grade projects .

Testing both, coding and coordination skills, LoC is split into 2 rounds. So, gear up and use this platform to showcase your excellence in various programming domains.

Guidelines

  1. Teams can be from one to four members.
  2. The reporting time is sharp 8:30am on the 22nd of February.
  3. Lunch,Dinner and Breakfast will be provided.
  4. All participants are requested to get their respective Identity Cards.
  5. Teams will be shortlisted on the basis of their Devfolio profile.
  6. All participants are requested to carry atleast one extension board per team.
  7. All coders are requested to get their laptops. The solution must be developed during the event only.
  8. Selected teams will receive a message from ACM by 18th of February and must respond to it within 2 days. Only those teams are requested to be present at the venue on the 22nd of February at 8:30 am.

FAQ

A hackathon is a design sprint-like event in which computer programmers and others involved in software development, including graphic designers, interface designers, project managers, and others collaborate intensively on software projects The goal of a hackathon is to create usable software with the goal of creating a functioning product by the end of the event.

Each team would comprise of 2-4 members including the team leader. All team members should be from same college; no inter-college teams are allowed. However, members from different branches of the same college/ institute can form a team.

LOC Hackathon is the best way to start as we provide you with mentors and talks from industry experts. Our aim is to provide a place to learn in a short amount of time, interact and learn from other like minded people.

Food, water and refreshments, prizes, internship and placement opportunities, A/C rooms and bedding, desktop computers, LAN connection for each member.

We recommend to get your own laptop with all the necessary softwares installed, chargers, student ID, change of clothes, pillow and blankets as per your convenience.

Weapons, drugs, alcohol of any kind are not permitted.
